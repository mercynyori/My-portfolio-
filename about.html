About Me

I got enthusiastic with Computer Science the day I realized I wanted to be a doctor. I was fascinated by the idea of how technology could help in the medical field and how I could help people live healthier lives even if it was just from a long distance point of view or even through a screen.

I wanted to make something that will meet the point of need even to the sick. I started learning programming languages like HTML/CSS and I was amazed by how I could create entire worlds through coding. I take pride in my role as a young lady in Computer Science to encourage other women to pursue the same.

Personally, a lady who loves fashion especially bags , I sometimes wish I had a personal advisor that would help me choose the right bag to match my outfits.So I decided to create one! I also like traveling and exploring new places, it always like a source of inspiration and a way of recharging my creativity.
